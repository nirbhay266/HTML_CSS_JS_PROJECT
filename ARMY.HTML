Army Agniveer Rally Eligibility 2024

Agniveer in General Duty GD, Technical, Assistant, Store Keeper, Tradesman (Men)
  • Agniveer Army Recruitment Candidate must be a resident of the district from where the rally is going to apply for recruitment.
  • Education Qualification : Class 8th, 10th, 12th Post Wise Eligibility.
  • Physical Eligibility Group I:
  • Height :
  • For Agniveer Office Assistant / Store Keeper : 162 CM
  • For All other Agniveer Post (GD, Technical / Tradesman) : 169 CM
  • 1.6 KM Running : 5 Min 30 Second – 60 Marks
  • Pull Ups : 10 Times – 40 Marks
  • 9 Feet Ditch
  • Zigzag Balance
  • More Details Read the Army Rally Notification.

Agniveer in General Duty GD (Women Military Police)

(Women)

  • Agniveer Army Recruitment Candidate must be a resident of the State from where the rally is going to apply for recruitment.
  • Education Qualification : Class 10th Matric Exam Passed with Minimum 45% Marks and 33% in Each Subjects.
  • Physical Eligibility Group I:
  • Height : 162 CMS
  • 1.6 KM Running : 7 Min 30 Second
  • 10 Feet Long Jump
  • 3 Feet High Jump
  • More Details Read the Army Rally Notification.

Soldier Technical Nursing Assistant
  • Agniveer Army Recruitment Candidate must be a resident of the State from where the rally is going to apply for recruitment.
  • Education Qualification : 10+2 Intermediate with Science with Physics, Chemistry, Biology and English with minimum 50% Marks and 40% in Each Subject OR 10+2 Intermediate in Science Stream with Physics, Chemistry, Botany, Zoology and English with 50% Marks and 40% in Each Subjects.
  • Physical Eligibility:
  • Height :
  • For Eastern Uttar Pradesh : 169 CM
  • For Western Uttar Pradesh : 170 CMS
  • Uttarakhand : 163 CMS
  • 1.6 KM Running : 5 Min 45 Second
  • Pull Ups :
  • 9 Feet Ditch
  • Zigzag Balance
  • More Details Read the Army Rally Notification.

Sepoy Pharma
  • Agniveer Army Recruitment Candidate must be a resident of the State from where the rally is going to apply for recruitment.
  • Education Qualification : 10+2 Exam Passed and Qualified in D.Pharma with Minimum 55% Marks and Pharmacy Council Registration OR B.Pharma with 50% Marks and Registered in State Pharmacy Council .
  • Physical Eligibility:
  • Height :
  • For Eastern Uttar Pradesh : 169 CM
  • For Western Uttar Pradesh : 170 CMS
  • Uttarakhand : 163 CMS
  • 1.6 KM Running : 5 Min 45 Second
  • Pull Ups :
  • 9 Feet Ditch
  • Zigzag Balance
  • More Details Read the Army Rally Notification.

JCO Religious Teacher (Dharm Guru) Pandit, Pandit Gorkha, Granthi, Maulvi, Padre, Bodh Monk
  • Education Qualification :
  • RT Pandit and Pandi Gorakha : Hindu Candidate with Shastri / Acharya in Sanskrit, (Karam Kand) as Main / Core Subject OR one Year Diploma in Karam Kand
  • RT Granthi : Sikh Candidate with Bachelor Degree in Any Stream, Gyani in Punjabi
  • RT Maulvi : Muslim Candidate : Bachelor Degree in Any Stream, Alim in Arabic or Adeeb-e-Mahir/ Urdu Mahir in Urdu
  • RT Padre : Christian Candidate with Bachelor Degree in Any Stream
  • RT Budhist : Budhist Candidate with Bachelor Degree in Any Stream
  • Physical Eligibility Group I:
  • Height :
  • General : 160 CM , Gorkhas & Ladakhi Region : 157 CM
  • Chest : 77 CM
  • 1.6 Km Running : 8 Min
  • More Details Read the Army Rally Notification.
