Post Date / Update: 27 February 2024 | 01:45 PM
Short Information : National Testing Agency (NTA) Common University Admission Test CUET UG 2024 is a common entrance examination conducted for admission to graduation courses in various universities of India. This year the entrance exam of CUET UG 2024 is held from 15 to 31 May 2024. Read the notification for CUET UG 2024 list of universities, course information, information brochure, further limit, syllabus and other information.

NTA CUET UG Exam 2024 : Short Details of Notification

Important Dates

  • Application Begin : 27/02/2024
  • Last Date for Apply Online : 26/03/2024
  • Last Date Online Payment : 26/03/2024
  • Correction Date : 28-29 March 2024
  • Exam Date : 15-31 May 2024
  • Exam City Available : 30/04/2024
  • Admit Card Available : Before Exam
  • Result Declared : 30/06/2024

Application Fee

  • For First 3 Subjects :
  • General : 1000/-
  • EWS / OBC : 900/-
  • SC / ST / PH : 800/-
  • For Add Additional Subject :
  • General : 400/- Each
  • EWS / OBC : 375/- Each
  • SC / ST / PH : 350/- Each
  • Pay the Examination Fee Through Online Debit Card, Credit Card or Net Banking Fee Mode
